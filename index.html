Hi I'm Eugene, a specialist bird guide focusing on trips throughout Southern Africa. I am here to help you find the spectacular birds that we share this world with. Our birding safaris are about learning new things, having fun and caring for nature.

Learning new things

Our main focus is seeing various bird species and locating the endemics. On our guided safaris you will also learn about different habitats, behaviours and calls. I'm also a videographer and photographer (you can check out my work here) so if you need any help with camera tips and tricks do not hesitate to let me know. Birding Safaris also provide personalised bird lists for all our trips.



Purple-Crested Turaco, Lake Malawi, Malawi

Having fun

One of the best things about birding is having time to forget the stresses in life, bring yourself into the present moment and really take in your surroundings. It's also an amazing feeling when you spot that new bird or new behaviour for the first time! Our safaris are also an ideal time to spend time with your favourite people and make some memories. Birding Safaris will organise and plan everything for your trip, visiting some of the most beautiful locations.


African Penguin, Betty's Bay, South Africa

Caring for nature

We contribute 10 % of profits to conservation and local communities, as we are passionate about birding and also about genuinely helping people and conserving birds for many generations to come.


Southern Masked Weaver, Kgalagadi, South Africa

About

Eugene's favourite things to do when growing up were climbing trees and taking trips to the nearby Kruger National Park. He studied Field Guiding in Limpopo and then moved to the South Luangwa National Park in Zambia. Here he became the manager of a beautiful and remote bush camp. He has also spent time Field Guiding in the Kruger National Park and started a Video Production company with his partner.
